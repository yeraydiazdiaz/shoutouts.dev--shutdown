shoutouts.dev BETA

shoutouts.dev has closed down

After about 18 months since launch it's clear to me the site simply doesn't appeal to the wider OSS developer audience. Perhaps due to my own shortcomings as a marketer or graphic designer, or maybe developers are just more used to sign praises via Twitter.

Whatever the case may be it's been collecting technical debt and hosting bills while having virtually no visits or engagement and I'm simply not motivated anymore so I've decided to close it down.

It's been a great learning experience and I'm grateful to everyone that registered their projects and left shoutouts. If you would like to me to send you the shoutouts for your project(s) let me know.
